Ages 0-4

Infant & Early Learning

Building the foundation for a lifetime of curiosity, creativity, and capability

The First Years Matter Most

In these crucial early years, children learn through every interaction, every sensation, and every moment of connection. Our curriculum respects the natural development of the infant brain while gently introducing the building blocks of future skills.

Core Learning Domains

Sensory Exploration

  • Texture baskets with natural materials
  • Sound exploration with safe instruments
  • Light and shadow play
  • Water and sand sensory bins
  • Scent exploration with herbs
  • Temperature discovery (warm/cold)

Movement & Body Awareness

  • Tummy time with interesting objects
  • Crawling obstacle courses
  • Baby yoga and stretching
  • Dance and rhythm games
  • Outdoor exploration on safe surfaces
  • Climbing and balance practice

Nature Connection

  • Daily outdoor time regardless of weather
  • Planting and watering simple seeds
  • Observing insects and birds
  • Collecting leaves and rocks
  • Feeling different tree barks
  • Listening to outdoor sounds

Language & Communication

  • Sign language for babies
  • Conversational narration throughout day
  • Multi-language exposure through songs
  • Storytelling with expression
  • Picture book exploration
  • Rhyming and finger plays

Developmental Milestones Through Play

0-12 Months

Responds to voices, reaches for objects, begins to explore with mouth and hands, shows curiosity about surroundings

12-24 Months

First words, walking, imitating actions, simple problem-solving, beginning of pretend play

24-48 Months

Complex sentences, imaginative play, helping with simple tasks, asking questions constantly, building with purpose
