The Betrayal: How America Failed Its Children

1983

"A Nation at Risk" - The Warning We Ignored

The National Commission on Excellence in Education declared: "The educational foundations of our society are being eroded by a rising tide of mediocrity that threatens our very future as a Nation and a people." They warned that if another country had done this to us, we'd view it as an act of war. We did nothing.

Source: U.S. Department of Education, 1983

2002

No Child Left Behind - The Testing Disaster

Schools began teaching to the test. Art, music, shop class, home economics - eliminated. Children became data points. Creativity died. By 2007, 62% of school districts had cut time from other subjects to focus on reading and math.

Source: Center on Education Policy, 2008

2015

Every Student Succeeds Act - More of the Same

Despite promises of change, the damage was done. A generation had grown up without ever using a tool, planting a seed, or fixing anything. We created excellent test-takers and helpless humans.

2024

The Crisis Today

54% of U.S. adults read below 6th grade level. 66% can't perform basic car maintenance. 89% of young adults wish they'd learned practical skills in school. We have failed an entire generation.
